THE HOLE IN THE WALL

Who: Biblos

What: Lebanese Cuisine

Where: West London

Why? #latenight #snacks #easyeats #wraps

Recommended: Chip Roll £1.50 or Lamb and Cheese Bread

This is a place I have been coming to since I was in Secondary School. It is quick, easy and always so tasty. The place is separated in two, one side is an open cafe and the other is a bakery fit with a clay stone oven.

In the bakery you can eat flatbreads made with minced lamb, cheese, spinach or zaatar for a mere £3-4.

In the main cafe there is a variety of goods to choose from but I thoroughly reccommedn thheir wraps. These range from £1.50 to £3.00. You can choose from all sorts of delicious fillings, but the following is what I go with. Chip Roll with Houmous, Garlic Sauce and Salad. They are delicious and my wraps are always filled to the brim and served by attentive and friendly people.
